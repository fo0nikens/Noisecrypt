Collections

One annoying thing in most social network is that you find a lot of useless information in your newsfeed. Charme allows you to subscribe to selected topics of a user only. This means you can subscribe to your boss' company information, but you do not have to subscribe to his familiy photos, for example.

Semantic Aggregation

When storing information in text form only information gets lost. In Charme, semantic information can be added to posts and make sure the desired audience will find them later. Such information include free seats in your car, item offers and events for example.

Group Messaging

This has every social network right now. But in contrast to other instant messaging services we feature a wide range of outstanding, lovely designed, and extremly useful Oktoberfest icons like brezel, bavarian flag, hendl, beer, dirndl and lederhosen.

Encryption

Charme uses end-to-end encryption for user messages, private profile information and encrypted posts. This means not even the server that hosts your data can read your private data. The data is encrypted on your device and decripted on devices of users you shared the data with. To achieve this, client and server are two seperate projects that communicate via JSON to each other.

Distributed Servers

One great things about email is that everybody can setup his own server. You can do the same thing for Charme. Host your own server within the global Charme network or setup a private server somewhere in your companies intranet. There is no central server needed at all.

Open Source

Charme is licensed under GNU GPLv3, so the source code is fully available, which means everybody can check out how Charme works, make improvments, provide bugfixes or verify the security.

Responsive Design

You can use Charme on your tablet, on your smartphone or on your desktop PC.

Scalable

Charme uses MongoDB as a database - a fast and reliable document oriented database.

A minimalistic UI

Charme features a minimalistic UI, which was developed to lower the distraction level to a minimum. This means: No ads, no recommendations and no spam.
